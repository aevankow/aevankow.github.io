The 2026 [Northeast Natural History Conference](https://www.eaglehill.us/NENHC_2026/NENHC2026.shtml) took place in April in Burlington, Vermont.

On Saturday, one of the first sessions was "Tales of Change Through the Lens of Lichens". This session was organized by Jeremy Howland and included four oral presentations. 

![NENHC1](http://aevankow.github.io/images/NENHC1.jpeg)
Caroline Witherspoon presented "Highlighting Herbarium Gaps with Noteworthy Records of *Usnea*."

![NENHC2](http://aevankow.github.io/images/NENHC2.jpeg)
Jason Hafstad presented "A Rarity Assessment of New Jersey’s Lichens."

![NENHC3](http://aevankow.github.io/images/NENHC3.jpeg)
Jeremy Howland presented "Evidence for Large-Scale Epiphyte Declines in the NE Necessitate Incorporation into Invasive Species Management and Monitoring."
